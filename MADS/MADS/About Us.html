ABOUT US

About team:

The "MADS" team is a football team founded in 2023 which actively participates in amateur tournaments. This team brings together football fans with different experiences in the game, but with a common passion for the sport. The name "MADS" is an abbreviation symbolizing the names of the team's founders, Maxim Antonov and Daniel Smirnov.

The main sport for MADS is football and they participate in various tournaments. Two of them are LFDL and GHETTO Games. These tournaments provide the team with the opportunity to compete with other amateur teams, improve their skills and take part in exciting football matches.

"MADS" strive to improve their game, maintain team spirit and a friendly atmosphere within the team. This team continues to develop, gaining experience, proudly representing themselves and their sportsmanship.
